Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-11-06

Fret Wizard Mobile does not collect, store, or share personal data.

  • No ads, tracking, or analytics SDKs are used.
  • No account is required. We do not operate a backend service.
  • Audio is generated locally on-device using Tone.js; usage never leaves your device.
  • App settings and progress (if any) are stored locally on your device only.
  • In-app purchases (if offered) are processed by Google Play. We do not receive your payment information. Purchase status is stored locally and may be validated by the store.
  • The app does not load third‑party code from the network at runtime. All code, styles, and assets are bundled with the app. No personal data is transmitted by the app.

If you have questions, contact: support@fretwizard.app
